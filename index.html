Key insight: Under all realistic scenarios, the median offset remains below 50%. Gradual PM₂.₅ improvement (2%/yr) achieves only half the benefit of instantaneous AQG achievement (11.4% vs 20.0%), quantifying a "delay cost" of ~9 percentage points.

Methods Summary

  • Model: GEMM 5-COD (Burnett et al. 2018 PNAS)
  • Causes: IHD, Stroke, COPD, Lung Cancer, LRI (age-specific θ for IHD/Stroke)
  • TMREL: 2.4 μg/m³
  • Data: WHO GHE 2021 (2020 deaths), UN WPP 2024, World Bank PM₂.₅
  • Years: 2020 (baseline) → 2050 (projection)
  • Countries: 184 with complete data
  • Targets: WHO IT-1 (35), IT-2 (25), IT-3 (15), IT-4 (10), AQG (5 μg/m³)

GEMM functional form: ln[HR(C)] = θ · ln(z/α + 1) / [1 + exp(-(z-μ)/τ)], where z = max(0, C - 2.4)
